About me

I am a creative Email Marketing Specialist who got my start as a "Web Development Immersive" (WDI) student at General Assembly (GA) Sydney.

I believe in teamwork, collaboration and presentation as key values to drive product success, adoption and consumer satisfaction.

Since I was young, I have enjoyed exploring my creative side and have been searching for a career in which I can apply this since I have moved to Australia. Over the past few years, I gained an interest in web development, inspired somewhat by my days of editing myspace profiles in CSS. The idea of being able to be part of creating a product that users will directly interact with holds great appeal to me.

I finally applied to WDI 27 at GA which has driven my captivation for front end web development to the next level.
